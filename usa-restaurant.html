Our Heritage

A tradition of the morning table

USA Restaurant has been a cornerstone of Peru, Indiana — a place where neighbors become regulars and regulars become family. We believe breakfast is not just a meal; it's the ceremony that sets the tone for your entire day.

Our kitchen starts before dawn, preparing everything from scratch with the same dedication that has made us a beloved institution in Miami County. From our golden hash browns to our hand-crafted omelets, every plate carries the weight of that commitment.

Come as you are. Leave as you wish you always felt.

—What We Offer

More than a meal

I
Scratch Kitchen

Every batter mixed, every sauce stirred, every biscuit rolled by hand each morning. We don't open a package; we open our pantry.

II
Fresh Coffee, Always

Our coffee is brewed fresh every 20 minutes. Refills flow freely because a great breakfast deserves a great cup — as many times as you need.

III
Family Welcome

Children's menu, high chairs, and a staff that genuinely loves families. Sundays were made for coming here together.

IV
Local & Loyal

Proudly serving Peru and Miami County for years. We source locally when we can, and we give back to the community that made us.
